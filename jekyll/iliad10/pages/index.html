Iliad, Book 10

A presentation of the Greek text of the Tenth Book of the Homeric Iliad, Book *Kappa* (**Κ**), sometimes called the “Doloneia”, as it appears on the Venetus A Manuscript (Marciana Graecai> 454 = 822), as edited by the Homer Multitext Project.

With a facing and aligned translation by Casey Dué and Mary Ebbott.
